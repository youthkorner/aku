University Policies

Admission Policy

Al-Kawthar University is committed to a fair and transparent admission process. Applicants must meet the minimum academic requirements and submit all necessary documents before the deadline.

Academic Integrity Policy

The university upholds strict academic integrity standards. Cheating, plagiarism, and other dishonest practices will not be tolerated and may result in disciplinary action.

Refund Policy

Tuition fee refunds are available under certain circumstances as outlined in the student handbook. Requests must be submitted within the specified time frame.

Code of Conduct

All students are expected to behave respectfully and responsibly on campus. Violations of the code of conduct can lead to warnings, suspension, or expulsion.

General Conduct Policy

All students are expected to maintain academic integrity and respect for peers and faculty. Any form of misconduct, including cheating, plagiarism, and harassment, is strictly prohibited.

Attendance Policy

Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in each course. Failure to meet this requirement may lead to disqualification from exams.

Grading & Assessment

Continuous assessment is based on class participation, quizzes, assignments, midterms, and finals. Grading criteria are shared at the beginning of each course.

Fee & Refund Policy

Fees are non-refundable after the semester has commenced. Exceptions are handled on a case-by-case basis upon submission of an official request with documented proof.

IT & Digital Use Policy

All university-provided digital services must be used responsibly. Misuse of platforms or unauthorized access may result in disciplinary action.
