Admission Policy

We welcome students from all backgrounds. Admission is based on academic performance, entry tests, and interview evaluation. The admission process is fair, transparent, and merit-based.

  • Applicants must hold at least a high school certificate or equivalent.
  • All applicants are required to take an entry test.
  • Interviews are mandatory for final selection.
  • Admissions open twice a year: Spring and Fall.
