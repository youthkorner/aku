Quality Enhancement Cell (QEC)

The Quality Enhancement Cell (QEC) at Al-Kawthar University is dedicated to promoting and sustaining quality assurance practices across all academic and administrative functions.

Functions of QEC
  • Monitoring and reviewing academic programs
  • Ensuring quality in teaching and learning
  • Developing Self-Assessment Reports (SARs)
  • Facilitating accreditation and external review
  • Conducting student and faculty surveys
Objectives of QEC
  • Ensure quality standards in teaching, research, and administration.
  • Conduct periodic self-assessments and audits.
  • Facilitate continuous improvement and accreditation processes.
  • Encourage stakeholder feedback and participation.
QEC Activities

QEC organizes workshops, seminars, and training sessions to enhance faculty and staff capabilities. It also collects feedback from students and alumni to improve the academic environment.
